Scope of Work

Clinical cleaning routines designed for healthcare environments.

Clinical and medical office cleaning from Zachâ€™s Quality Services Inc follows structured routines, written procedures and infection-conscious protocols. Below is a representative scope of work, adapted to your practice type, risk level and regulatory expectations.

  • Exam & treatment rooms: Clean and disinfect exam tables, counters, sinks, high-touch surfaces and frequently used equipment exteriors according to your guidelines.
  • Waiting rooms & reception: Wipe and disinfect chairs, armrests, check-in counters, door handles and other patient contact points; maintain floors and glass.
  • Restrooms: Detailed cleaning and disinfection of toilets, urinals, sinks and partitions; refill paper, soap and sanitizer; maintain odor control.
  • High-touch disinfection: Focused treatment of door hardware, railings, elevator buttons, switches, keypad areas and shared workstations.
  • Nurse stations & staff areas: Clean common surfaces, break rooms and staff restrooms while observing privacy and security instructions.
  • Floors: Sweep and mop hard surfaces with appropriate solutions; vacuum carpet and rugs; spot clean visible stains in patient and staff areas.
  • Waste handling: Consolidate general waste and recycling according to your policies, coordinating with your regulated medical waste provider as needed.

The exact scope for your clinic may include added services such as terminal cleans for specific rooms, scheduled deep cleaning, high dusting, floor refinishing or expanded disinfection protocols depending on your specialty and occupancy.
