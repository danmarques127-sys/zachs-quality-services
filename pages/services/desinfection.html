Scope of Work

Sanitization procedures designed for high-risk areas & frequent use.

Our disinfection & sanitization process includes targeted methods based on building type, occupancy level and surface exposure. Below is a typical scope of work that can be customized for your facility:

  • High-touch surfaces: Door hardware, elevator buttons, railings, light switches, break room counters and reception desks sanitized using approved disinfectants.
  • Spray application: Electrostatic or manual sprayers used for wider coverage, following recommended dwell times and PPE guidelines.
  • Restrooms & staff areas: Detailed cleaning and disinfection of shared spaces to reduce risk of cross-contamination.
  • Clinical-level procedures: Available for medical offices, labs and healthcare environments when required by policy or regulation.
  • Odor & air quality support: Optional deodorizing or IAQ improvement treatments depending on facility type.
  • Material protection: Each product is chosen for compatibility with surface materials, ensuring no damage or residue is left behind.

Our program can be added to your nightly, weekly or day porter routines, or scheduled as a standalone service during flu season or after exposure events. PPE, MSDS sheets and product documentation provided upon request.
