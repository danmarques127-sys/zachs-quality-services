Scope of Work

Strip & wax procedures to restore and protect your floors.

Our strip & wax / refinishing process follows tested procedures, ensuring each step is performed safely and effectively according to the floor type. Below is a standard scope of work, adaptable to your building and coating requirements.

  • Floor inspection: Identification of coating condition, material type, wear zones and restoration level required.
  • Protective barriers: Furniture masking, corner protection and walk-off zones secured before work begins.
  • Chemical stripping: Application of commercial-grade stripper to dissolve old finish layers.
  • Machine scrub & removal: Agitation with floor machines followed by wet vacuum recovery.
  • Neutral rinse: Balanced pH rinse to prepare surface for new finish application.
  • Finish application: Multiple coats of sealer or wax, applied evenly with controlled drying times.
  • Final inspection: Supervisor confirms uniformity, coating thickness and walkability before area is reopened.

We also offer periodic maintenance plans including buffing, recoating, burnishing, grout cleaning and water recovery as needed for your facility type and occupancy level.
