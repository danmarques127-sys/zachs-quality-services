Scope of Work

Post-construction cleaning phases for a clean, ready space.

Post-construction clean-up from Zach‚Äôs Quality Services Inc typically follows a phased process. The exact scope is adjusted to your project size, materials and schedule, but commonly includes:

  • Debris removal: Consolidation and removal of loose construction debris, packaging, plastic, cardboard and non-hazardous materials.
  • Rough clean: Initial sweep and vacuum of floors; removal of visible dust from ledges, baseboards and exposed surfaces.
  • Detail dust removal: High and low dusting of vents, fixtures, trim, door frames, window sills and built-in millwork.
  • Glass & frames: Cleaning of interior glass, entrance doors and side lights; removal of stickers, tape residue and smudges.
  • Surface cleaning: Wiping and sanitizing of counters, shelves, cabinets and hard surfaces according to material guidelines.
  • Restrooms & break areas: Full cleaning and disinfection of newly installed fixtures, partitions and appliances; supply set-up if requested.
  • Floor finishing: Final sweeping, mopping, vacuuming and, where applicable, machine cleaning or initial floor care treatments.
  • Final walk-through support: Touch-ups prior to punch-list visits, ownership tours or tenant move-in dates.

Larger projects may schedule multiple visits at different construction phases. Our team can coordinate directly with your general contractor or facility manager to align cleaning with inspections and handover dates.
